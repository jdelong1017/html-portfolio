The Top 4 Best TV Shows According to Jason

Heartstopper

I love this show because it's the first show that I was able to see my young queer self in.

Grey's Anatomy

I have an emotional and spiritual connection to this show. I can watch it over and over again and never get bored of it.

How to Get Away with Murder

This show brings out the best acting in Viola Davis. She shows the rawness of Analise Keating in a way that's never been done by a woman of color on mainstream television. It's ICONIC!

Scandal

Olivia Pope is BADASS!! Need I say more?
